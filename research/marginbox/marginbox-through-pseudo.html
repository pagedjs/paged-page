Margin boxes

Content is inserted with CSS, using the ::part() & ::before pseudo-selectors.

paged-horizontal-margin-through-pseudo exposes four parts:

  1. left the left marginbox
  2. center the center marginbox
  3. right the right marginbox
  4. margin-box all the marginboxes at once. Rename to all?

The part margin-box allows to style all margin-boxes at once.
